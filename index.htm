  • Biryani recipe

    Afghani biryani, also known as Qabuli pulao, is a tasty rice dish from Afghanistan. It is made of
    spices, rice and meat. The name 'biryani' is derived from the Persian word 'beriya(n)' meaning fried or roasted.
    Biryani originated in Iran (Persia) and was brought to the Indian subcontinent by merchants
    and travelers. Local variants of the dish are extremely popular in South Asia as well as Arabia.
